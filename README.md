# Personal Solutions to CS:APP Labs

## Introduction

**C**omputer **S**ystems: **A** **P**rogrammer's **P**erspective, 3/E (CS:APP3e), is a well-known book authored by Randal E. Bryant and David R. O'Hallaron.
It's corresponding lectures in Carnegie Mellon University as CMU 15-213 are also famous and gorgeous. This book & lectures provide a collective perspective from programmer.
This repository contains my personal solutions to labs in these lectures, which can be found in [official site](https://csapp.cs.cmu.edu/3e/labs.html).

Information and archives of labs are seperated to each corresponding folder, which could be downloaded from the site above through `Self-Study Handout` trailing at the bullet
of lab titles. Also, my thoughts and small talks on these labs are available on my [personal site](https://azusagawa-sakuta.github.io/codes/CSAPP/CSAPP/).

It's too hard to write report such like this for me, but one more thing, these labs have high tech contents and deserved to make your hands dirty. Have fun and good luck!
